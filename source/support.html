Frequently asked questions

Things are broken for me, where can I find help?

Please don't hesitate to contact us by email at info@olery.com. However, if you have problems with a specific component, you can also go to the Github repository of the component and create an issue in the issue tracker.


Can I use all of this for free?

Most of it, yes. We created a license overview to indicate which components are free for all, and which components are only available for non-commercial or research use.


Can I adapt OpeNER for my own domain?

Yes you can, theres a getting started guide available on how to adapt opener to your own domain over at the gettings started section of this portal.
